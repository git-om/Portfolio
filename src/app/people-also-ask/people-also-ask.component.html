People Also Ask For

To love or to be loved, you'll need to love yourself. If you don't know how to love yourself, you cannot give or take the love that you really love.

Thinking like a sherlock will not make you wise. You know what, every little thing, every single person that happens in your life is always trying to teach you something. Experience it, question yourself, take the knowledge out of it. If you can show the experience with good judgement, I think you are more than wise. Never ignore any moment or any person in life.

Success is winning over your mind. To be able to understand what you are and rejoice what you have. To be able to say sorry from the bottom of your heart. To be able to take the full responsibility of your own actions. Success is not to lie to yourself.
